About us

We are a group of Smart Investors committed to building a platform to connect students with mutual interest in finance and investment and provide them the opportunity to exchange ideas and share insights. We are here to showcase your talent and introduce you to new friends and professionals in the field of finance. Our Stories

Feburary 2017

Five University of Toronto Rotman Commerce students got together and came up with the idea of Smart Investors to build a platform for students who are either curious or passionate about finance and investment to connect and share ideas with each other.

August 2017

Our first online trading competition was launched over the summer with over 100 participants.

October 2018

Our Equity Research Committee was formed for students who displayed extensive interest in conducting research and analysis on specific industry or company. At the end of each school year, members would give a stock pitch presentation to showcase their results.
